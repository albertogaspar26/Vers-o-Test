What We Do

Considered care, from soil to bloom.

Two dedicated disciplines — stewardship and organic lawn care — supported by design and consulting expertise.

Garden Stewardship

Ongoing, hands-on care — seasonal maintenance, pruning, and plant health monitoring that keeps every bed and border thriving year-round.

Organic Lawn Care

Eco-conscious lawn treatments built around soil health — free from harsh chemicals, safe for children, pets, and the wider ecosystem.

Landscape Design

Thoughtful garden plans shaped by your property's architecture, light, and native ecology — designed to mature gracefully.

Horticultural Consulting

Expert guidance on plant selection, siting, and long-term landscape management for properties that deserve real expertise.
